Overview

Detroit: Become Human, the latest and most ambitious video game production from Quantic Dream, is finally coming to PC! Featuring world-renowned actors like Jesse Williams (Grey’s Anatomy), Clancy Brown (Carnivale), Lance Henriksen (Aliens), Bryan Dechart (True Blood), and Valorie Curry (Twilight), the game places the destiny of both mankind and androids in your hands. Enter a near future where every choice you make shapes the outcome of an intricately branching narrative.

Key Features

  • Gripping Narrative: Discover a world where moral dilemmas and difficult decisions can turn android slaves into revolutionaries.
  • Your Choices Matter: Shape the fate of the three main characters and the entire city of Detroit.
  • Countless Endings: Replay again and again to discover unique outcomes.
  • Fully Optimized for PC: Experience stunning graphics, 4K resolution, and smooth 60 fps gameplay.

Specifications

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 10 (64 bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-2300 @ 2.8 GHz or Ryzen 3 1200 @ 3.1GHz
  • Memory: 8GB RAM
  • Video: Nvidia GeForce GTX 780 or AMD HD 7950 (Vulkan 1.1 required)
  • Video RAM: 3GB or more
  • Hard Drive: 55 GB

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 11 (64 bit)
  • Processor: Intel i5-6600 @ 3.3 GHz or Ryzen 3 1300 X @ 3.4 GHz
  • Memory: 12GB RAM
  • Video: Nvidia GeForce GTX 1060 or AMD Radeon RX 580 (Vulkan 1.1 required)
  • Video RAM: 4GB or more
  • Hard Drive: 55 GB
